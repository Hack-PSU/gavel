Judging Guide

Questions to Ask

  1. What inspired your idea/project?
  2. How did you decide on your approach?
  3. How did you make sure your solution is usable for the target audience?
  4. What did you learn during your project (technical/nontechnical)?
  5. What was a challenge you faced working on this project?
  6. How did your team handle areas where no one had prior experience?
  7. How did you divide the work among your team?
  8. What makes your project different from competitors or other existing solutions?
  9. If you had more time, what features would you add/improve?

Evaluation Metrics

  1. Creativity and Originality: How original and innovative is the project?
  2. Impact: What impact does this project have?
  3. Implementation: How well is the project executed?
  4. Clarity: How clear is the presentation and documentation?
  5. Knowledge and Growth: How much learning and growth is demonstrated?
